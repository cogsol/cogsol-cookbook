# Beef Tacos

Flavorful seasoned ground beef served in warm corn tortillas with fresh toppings. A quick and satisfying Mexican classic that the whole family can customize to their taste.

## Ingredients

### Beef Filling
- 500g ground beef
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 60ml water

### Toppings
- 12 small corn tortillas
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup diced white onion
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- Fresh cilantro leaves
- 1 avocado, sliced
- Sour cream
- Hot sauce or salsa
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it into small pieces, about 6 minutes. Drain excess fat.
2. Add the diced onion and garlic. Cook for 2 minutes until the onion softens.
3. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper. Add the water and simmer for 5 minutes until the mixture thickens.
4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over a gas flame for about 30 seconds per side.
5. Spoon the seasoned beef into the warm tortillas.
6. Let everyone add their preferred toppings: lettuce, tomatoes, onion, cheese, cilantro, avocado, sour cream, salsa, and a squeeze of lime.

## Details

- Preparation time: 10 minutes
- Cooking time: 15 minutes
- Difficulty: Easy
- Servings: 4
- Cuisine: Mexican
- Type: Main course
