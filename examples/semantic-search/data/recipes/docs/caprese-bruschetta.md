# Caprese Bruschetta

Toasted bread topped with fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and fragrant basil, finished with a drizzle of balsamic glaze. A simple Italian appetizer that showcases the beauty of quality ingredients.

## Ingredients

- 1 baguette or Italian bread, sliced into 1cm rounds
- 3 ripe tomatoes, diced
- 200g fresh mozzarella, diced
- Fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon balsamic glaze
- 1 clove garlic, halved
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 200C. Arrange the bread slices on a baking tray.
2. Brush each slice lightly with olive oil. Toast in the oven for 5 minutes until golden and crispy.
3. Remove from the oven and immediately rub each slice with the cut side of the garlic clove while still warm.
4. In a bowl, gently combine the diced tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. Drizzle with the remaining olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Toss lightly.
5. Spoon the tomato-mozzarella mixture generously onto each toast.
6. Drizzle with balsamic glaze just before serving.
7. Serve immediately while the bread is still crispy.

## Details

- Preparation time: 10 minutes
- Cooking time: 5 minutes
- Difficulty: Easy
- Servings: 4 (about 16 pieces)
- Cuisine: Italian
- Type: Appetizer
