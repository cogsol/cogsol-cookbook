# Lentil Curry

A hearty, protein-rich vegetarian curry made with red lentils simmered in a fragrant blend of spices, tomatoes, and coconut milk. Comforting and budget-friendly, this one-pot dish is perfect for meal prep.

## Ingredients

- 250g red lentils, rinsed
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil or vegetable oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
- 1 tablespoon curry powder
- 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
- 400g canned diced tomatoes
- 400ml coconut milk
- 500ml vegetable broth
- 200g fresh spinach
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Fresh cilantro for garnish
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Steamed basmati rice for serving

## Instructions

1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until translucent.
2. Add the garlic and ginger. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and cayenne pepper. Toast the spices for 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
4. Add the diced tomatoes, coconut milk, and vegetable broth. Stir to combine.
5. Add the rinsed lentils. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the lentils are tender and the curry has thickened.
6. Stir in the spinach and cook for 2 minutes until wilted.
7. Add the lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Taste and adjust seasoning.
8. Serve over steamed basmati rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.

## Details

- Preparation time: 10 minutes
- Cooking time: 30 minutes
- Difficulty: Easy
- Servings: 4
- Cuisine: Indian
- Type: Main course (vegetarian)
