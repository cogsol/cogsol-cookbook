# Lemon Garlic Shrimp

Juicy shrimp sauteed in a buttery lemon-garlic sauce with a hint of white wine. Ready in under 15 minutes, this elegant dish works as a quick dinner or an impressive appetizer.

## Ingredients

- 500g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 60ml dry white wine (or chicken broth)
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Crusty bread or pasta for serving

## Instructions

1. Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of red pepper flakes.
2. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add the shrimp in a single layer. Cook for 1 minute per side until pink and curled. Remove to a plate.
4. In the same skillet, reduce heat to medium. Add the remaining butter and garlic. Cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
5. Pour in the white wine and lemon juice. Let it simmer for 2 minutes, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.
6. Return the shrimp to the skillet. Add the lemon zest and parsley. Toss everything together for 30 seconds.
7. Serve immediately with crusty bread to soak up the sauce, or toss with pasta.

## Details

- Preparation time: 10 minutes
- Cooking time: 5 minutes
- Difficulty: Easy
- Servings: 4
- Cuisine: Mediterranean
- Type: Main course
