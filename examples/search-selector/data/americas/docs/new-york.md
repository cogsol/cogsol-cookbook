# New York City, United States

The city that never sleeps offers world-class museums, iconic skylines, Broadway theater, and neighborhoods with flavors from every corner of the globe.

## Overview

New York City is the most populous city in the United States, spread across five boroughs at the mouth of the Hudson River. Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island each have distinct identities. As a global center of finance, media, art, and fashion, NYC attracts millions of visitors every year.

## Top Attractions

- **Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island**: Iconic symbols of freedom and the American immigrant experience.
- **Central Park**: An 843-acre urban oasis in the heart of Manhattan with meadows, lakes, and walking paths.
- **Times Square**: A dazzling neon-lit hub of Broadway theaters, shops, and street performers.
- **Metropolitan Museum of Art**: One of the world's largest and finest art collections spanning 5,000 years.
- **Brooklyn Bridge**: A historic suspension bridge offering stunning views of the Manhattan skyline.

## Local Cuisine

New York is a food capital with something for every palate. Grab a dollar slice of pizza folded in half, a loaded bagel with lox and cream cheese, dim sum in Chinatown, or a pastrami sandwich from a classic deli. Food halls like Chelsea Market and Smorgasburg in Brooklyn showcase the city's diverse culinary scene.

## Best Time to Visit

April to June and September to November offer comfortable weather and pleasant conditions for walking. Summer is hot and humid but alive with outdoor festivals. Winter brings holiday decorations, ice skating rinks, and the magic of a snowy Central Park.

## Travel Tips

- Buy an unlimited MetroCard for subway and bus rides if staying more than a few days.
- Walk as much as possible; many of Manhattan's best sights are within walking distance of each other.
- Make Broadway show reservations in advance or try the TKTS booth in Times Square for same-day discounts.
- Avoid eating in Times Square restaurants; better and cheaper options are just a few blocks away.
- Tipping 18-20% is customary at restaurants and expected for most services.
