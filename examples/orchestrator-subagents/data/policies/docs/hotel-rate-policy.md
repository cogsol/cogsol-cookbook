# Hotel Rate Policy

## Maximum Nightly Rates by City Tier

### Tier 1 Cities (New York, London, Tokyo, Paris, Zurich)
- Maximum: EUR 200/night
- Corporate rate preferred: check availability before booking at rack rate

### Tier 2 Cities (Madrid, Barcelona, Berlin, Amsterdam, Milan, Sydney)
- Maximum: EUR 150/night
- Corporate rate preferred: check availability before booking at rack rate

### Tier 3 Cities (all other destinations)
- Maximum: EUR 120/night

## Booking Requirements
- Use corporate-rate hotels when available (rates negotiated with NH, Marriott, Accor chains)
- If no corporate rate is available, book the most reasonably priced option within the nightly limit
- Extended stays (5+ nights): contact travel desk for negotiated rates

## Room Type
- Standard single or double room
- Suites are not covered unless the rate is within the standard room limit
- Room upgrades at personal expense are permitted

## Additional Charges
- WiFi: reimbursable if not included in the room rate
- Parking: reimbursable when public transport is not practical
- Minibar and in-room entertainment: not reimbursable
- Laundry: reimbursable for trips of 5+ nights

## Cancellation
- Cancel at least 24 hours before check-in to avoid no-show charges
- No-show fees are not reimbursable unless due to flight delays (provide documentation)
