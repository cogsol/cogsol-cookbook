# Flight Booking Policy

## Class of Service

### Domestic and Short-Haul Flights (under 6 hours)
- All employees: Economy class
- Exception: VP level and above may book Business class

### Long-Haul Flights (6 hours or more)
- Junior staff: Economy class (Premium Economy if available at less than 30% surcharge)
- Senior staff and managers: Premium Economy
- Directors and above: Business class

## Advance Booking Requirements
- Domestic flights: book at least 7 days in advance
- International flights: book at least 14 days in advance
- Last-minute bookings (less than 48 hours) require manager approval regardless of amount

## Preferred Airlines
- Use corporate-rate airlines when the fare difference is within 15% of the lowest available option
- Low-cost carriers are acceptable for short-haul routes under 3 hours

## Booking Limits
- Economy round trip: up to USD 1,500 (domestic), USD 3,000 (international)
- Business round trip: up to USD 5,000 (international only)
- Any fare exceeding these limits requires director-level approval

## Cancellation and Changes
- Book flexible fares when trip dates are uncertain
- Non-refundable fares are acceptable when dates are confirmed
- Cancellation fees are reimbursable with manager approval
