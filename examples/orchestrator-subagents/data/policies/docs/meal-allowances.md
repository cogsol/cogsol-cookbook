# Meal Allowances

## Per Diem Rates

### Domestic Travel
- Breakfast: EUR 15
- Lunch: EUR 20
- Dinner: EUR 30
- Daily total: EUR 65

### International Travel - Europe
- Breakfast: EUR 20
- Lunch: EUR 25
- Dinner: EUR 40
- Daily total: EUR 85

### International Travel - Americas
- Breakfast: USD 20
- Lunch: USD 30
- Dinner: USD 45
- Daily total: USD 95

### International Travel - Asia/Oceania
- Breakfast: USD 18
- Lunch: USD 25
- Dinner: USD 40
- Daily total: USD 83

## Rules
- Meals included in hotel, flight, or event registration are deducted from the per diem
- Alcohol is not reimbursable as a separate expense but is covered within the meal per diem
- Receipts are required for any individual meal exceeding EUR 30 / USD 35
- Tips are reimbursable up to 15% of the meal cost

## Business Meals with Clients
- Business meals with external clients may exceed per diem limits
- Maximum: EUR 75 / USD 85 per person
- Requires manager pre-approval for groups of 4 or more
- Receipt and attendee list required for all business meals
