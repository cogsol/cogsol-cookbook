# Expense Reporting

## Submission Deadlines
- Expense reports must be submitted within 10 business days of trip completion
- Late submissions (10-30 days) require manager justification
- Submissions after 30 days may not be reimbursed

## Required Documentation
- Original receipts for all expenses over EUR 25 / USD 30
- Digital photos of receipts are accepted
- Credit card statements alone are not sufficient as receipts
- Boarding passes for all flights (digital or physical)

## Report Contents
- Trip dates and destination
- Business purpose and outcomes
- Itemized expenses by category (transport, hotel, meals, other)
- Currency conversion rates used (use the rate from the date of the expense)
- Manager approval reference number

## Reimbursement
- Approved expenses are reimbursed within 15 business days
- Reimbursement is processed in the employee's local currency
- Foreign currency expenses are converted at the corporate exchange rate published monthly

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal entertainment and sightseeing
- Minibar and in-room entertainment
- First class upgrades (unless pre-approved)
- Gym or spa services (unless included in hotel rate)
- Traffic fines and parking tickets
- Travel insurance (company provides corporate coverage)

## Audits
- Expense reports are subject to random audits
- Retain all original receipts for 12 months after submission
- Fraudulent claims result in disciplinary action
