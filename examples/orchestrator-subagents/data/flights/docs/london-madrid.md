- **Origin**: London Heathrow (LHR) Terminal 5 (BA) / Terminal 3 (Iberia), London Stansted (STN) for Ryanair

## Booking Notes

- Short-haul route with high frequency and competitive pricing
- British Airways and Iberia codeshare on most flights
- Budget carriers offer significantly lower fares but with baggage restrictions
