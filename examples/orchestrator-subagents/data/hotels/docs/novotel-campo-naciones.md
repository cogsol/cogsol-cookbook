# Novotel Madrid Campo de las Naciones

## Location
- Address: Calle Amsterdam 3, 28042 Madrid
- District: Campo de las Naciones, near IFEMA convention center
- Nearby: IFEMA trade fair grounds, Parque Juan Carlos I
- Distance to airport: 10 minutes by car
- Distance to city center: 20 minutes by metro (Line 8)

## Room Rates
- Standard Room: EUR 110/night
- Executive Room: EUR 145/night
- Corporate rate: EUR 98/night (requires company agreement)

## Amenities
- Free high-speed WiFi
- Full-service restaurant and lobby bar
- Outdoor swimming pool (seasonal)
- Fitness center and sauna
- Conference facilities for up to 200 people
- Free shuttle to IFEMA
- Parking (EUR 15/day)

## Best For
- Business travelers attending events at IFEMA
- Guests looking for a full-service hotel with conference facilities
- Travelers who need proximity to the airport
