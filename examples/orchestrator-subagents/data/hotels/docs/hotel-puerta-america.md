# Hotel Puerta America Madrid

- Address: Avenida de America 41, 28002 Madrid
- District: Avenida de America, eastern business area
- Nearby: Avenida de America transport hub, Eisenhower business park
- Distance to airport: 15 minutes by car
- Distance to city center (Puerta del Sol): 10 minutes by metro (Lines 4, 6, 7, 9)

## Room Rates
- Standard Room: EUR 120/night
- Design Room: EUR 160/night
- Suite: EUR 250/night

- Free high-speed WiFi

- On-site restaurant and cocktail bar

- Rooftop pool (seasonal)
- Parking (EUR 22/day)
- Direct access to metro and bus station
