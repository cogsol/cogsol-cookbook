# Ibis Madrid Aeropuerto Barajas

## Location
- Address: Calle Campezo 4, 28022 Madrid
- District: Barajas, near the airport
- Nearby: Madrid-Barajas Airport terminals, Barajas metro station
- Distance to airport: 5 minutes by free shuttle
- Distance to city center: 30 minutes by metro (Line 8)

## Room Rates
- Standard Room: EUR 65/night
- No corporate rate program (already budget pricing)

## Amenities
- Free WiFi
- 24-hour snack bar
- Breakfast buffet (EUR 9)
- Parking (EUR 10/day)
- Free airport shuttle every 30 minutes
- 24-hour front desk

## Best For
- Budget-conscious travelers
- Early morning or late night flights
- Short overnight stays between connections
- Travelers who prioritize airport proximity over city access
