# The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful drama about hope and perseverance. Banker Andy Dufresne is wrongfully convicted of murder and sentenced to life at Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over two decades, he forms an unlikely friendship with fellow inmate Red while quietly working on a secret plan to reclaim his freedom. Through patience, intelligence, and an unbreakable spirit, Andy transforms not only his own fate but the lives of those around him.

## Details

- Year: 1994
- Director: Frank Darabont
- Genre: Drama
- Language: English
- Duration: 142 minutes
- Starring: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
