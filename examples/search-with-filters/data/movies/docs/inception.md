# Inception

A mind-bending science fiction thriller about the architecture of dreams. Dom Cobb is a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious during dream states. Offered a chance to have his criminal record erased, he takes on an impossible job: planting an idea deep within a target's mind, a process known as inception. Cobb assembles a team and constructs layered dream worlds, but his own buried memories threaten to collapse everything. A story about reality, memory, and letting go.

## Details

- Year: 2010
- Director: Christopher Nolan
- Genre: Sci-Fi
- Language: English
- Duration: 148 minutes
- Starring: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Elliot Page
