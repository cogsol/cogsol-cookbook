# Pulp Fiction

A genre-defining thriller told through interconnected stories of crime in Los Angeles. The film weaves together the tales of two hitmen on a job, a boxer paid to throw a fight, and a pair of small-time robbers, all colliding in unexpected ways. Known for its sharp dialogue, non-linear storytelling, and darkly comic tone, it redefined modern cinema and became a cultural landmark.

## Details

- Year: 1994
- Director: Quentin Tarantino
- Genre: Thriller
- Language: English
- Duration: 154 minutes
- Starring: John Travolta, Uma Thurman, Samuel L. Jackson
