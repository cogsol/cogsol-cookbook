# Spirited Away

A magical animated adventure from Studio Ghibli. Ten-year-old Chihiro stumbles into a mysterious world of spirits and gods after her parents are transformed into pigs by a powerful witch named Yubaba. To survive and rescue her family, Chihiro must work in Yubaba's bathhouse, navigating a strange realm filled with fantastical creatures, dangerous magic, and unexpected kindness. A coming-of-age story about courage, identity, and growing up.

## Details

- Year: 2001
- Director: Hayao Miyazaki
- Genre: Animation
- Language: Japanese
- Duration: 125 minutes
- Starring: Rumi Hiiragi, Miyu Irino
