# The Grand Budapest Hotel

A whimsical comedy set in a fictional European country between the World Wars. The film follows Gustave H., the legendary concierge of the Grand Budapest Hotel, and Zero Moustafa, the lobby boy who becomes his most trusted companion. When Gustave is framed for the murder of a wealthy elderly guest, he and Zero embark on a wild adventure involving a stolen Renaissance painting, a family fortune dispute, and a secret society of hotel concierges. A visually stunning, fast-paced caper filled with wit and heart.

## Details

- Year: 2014
- Director: Wes Anderson
- Genre: Comedy
- Language: English
- Duration: 99 minutes
- Starring: Ralph Fiennes, Tony Revolori, F. Murray Abraham
