# Pan's Labyrinth

A dark fantasy drama set in post-Civil War Spain, 1944. Young Ofelia moves with her pregnant mother to the countryside to live with her new stepfather, a cruel army captain hunting guerrilla fighters. Ofelia discovers an ancient labyrinth and meets a faun who tells her she is the reincarnation of a princess from an underground kingdom. She must complete three dangerous tasks to prove her identity, while the horrors of the real world close in around her. A haunting story that blurs the line between fairy tale and brutal reality.

## Details

- Year: 2006
- Director: Guillermo del Toro
- Genre: Drama
- Language: Spanish
- Duration: 118 minutes
- Starring: Ivana Baquero, Sergi Lopez, Doug Jones
