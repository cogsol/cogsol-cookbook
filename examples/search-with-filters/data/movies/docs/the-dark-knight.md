# The Dark Knight

An intense action film that elevated the superhero genre. Batman faces his greatest challenge when the Joker, a chaotic criminal mastermind, launches a campaign of terror across Gotham City. As the Joker manipulates everyone around him, Batman must decide how far he is willing to go to stop the madness. District Attorney Harvey Dent's tragic transformation adds moral complexity to a story about justice, sacrifice, and the thin line between hero and villain.

## Details

- Year: 2008
- Director: Christopher Nolan
- Genre: Action
- Language: English
- Duration: 152 minutes
- Starring: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart
