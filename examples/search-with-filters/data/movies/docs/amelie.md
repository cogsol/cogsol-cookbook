# Amelie

A charming French comedy about a shy waitress in Montmartre, Paris. Amelie Poulain discovers a childhood treasure hidden in her apartment and decides to return it to its owner. Inspired by the joy this brings, she embarks on a secret mission to improve the lives of those around her through elaborate, whimsical schemes. Along the way, she must find the courage to pursue her own happiness and connect with a quirky young man who collects discarded photo booth pictures.

## Details

- Year: 2001
- Director: Jean-Pierre Jeunet
- Genre: Comedy
- Language: French
- Duration: 122 minutes
- Starring: Audrey Tautou, Mathieu Kassovitz
