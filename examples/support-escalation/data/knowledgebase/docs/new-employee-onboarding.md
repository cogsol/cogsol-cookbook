# New Employee IT Onboarding

Complete IT setup guide for new employees.

## Day 1 Checklist

Your manager should have submitted an onboarding request before your start date. On your first day:

1. Collect your laptop and accessories from IT (Building A, Room 210).
2. Sign in with the temporary credentials provided in your welcome email.
3. Change your password immediately at https://password.company.com.
4. Set up Multi-Factor Authentication (see Security Best Practices guide).
5. Connect to the corporate Wi-Fi network "CorpNet-Secure".

## Essential Software

The following applications are pre-installed on your laptop:

- Microsoft Office 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams)
- GlobalProtect VPN client
- Company-approved web browser (Edge or Chrome)
- Adobe Acrobat Reader

Additional software can be installed from Software Center.

## Account Setup

- Email: Your email address is firstname.lastname@company.com.
- Intranet: https://intranet.company.com (bookmark this).
- Help Desk Portal: https://helpdesk.company.com (for IT requests).
- HR Portal: https://hr.company.com (for HR-related items).

## Access Requests

Some systems require additional access permissions:

1. Standard access is provisioned automatically based on your role.
2. For additional access, your manager must submit a request at the Help Desk Portal.
3. Access to sensitive systems requires security clearance approval.

## First Week Tasks

- Complete the IT Security Awareness training in the Learning Portal.
- Join the #it-announcements and #it-support channels in Teams.
- Test VPN access from home to confirm remote work readiness.
- Set up email on your mobile device (see the Mobile Email FAQ).

## Getting Help

If you run into any issues during your first week:

- Ask your onboarding buddy (assigned by your manager).
- Visit the IT Help Desk at Building A, Room 210.
- Submit a ticket at https://helpdesk.company.com.
- Chat with us on Teams at #it-support.
