# Creamy Tomato Soup

A smooth, velvety tomato soup made with roasted tomatoes, onions, and garlic. Finished with a touch of cream for richness. The ultimate comfort food, especially when paired with a grilled cheese sandwich.

## Ingredients

- 1kg ripe tomatoes, halved
- 1 large onion, quartered
- 4 cloves garlic, whole
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 500ml vegetable broth
- 100ml heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Fresh basil leaves
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 200C. Place the halved tomatoes, onion quarters, and garlic cloves on a baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
2. Roast for 30 minutes until the tomatoes are soft and slightly charred at the edges.
3. Transfer the roasted vegetables to a large pot. Add the vegetable broth, tomato paste, and sugar.
4. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes.
5. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until completely smooth. Alternatively, blend in batches in a regular blender.
6. Stir in the heavy cream. Taste and adjust seasoning.
7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.

## Details

- Preparation time: 10 minutes
- Cooking time: 40 minutes
- Difficulty: Easy
- Servings: 4
- Cuisine: American
- Type: Soup
